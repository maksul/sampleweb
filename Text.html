Chapter One

Molly has been staring out of her window about an one now. On her desk lying between a copy of Nature, New Scientist, and all the scientific journals her works has appaered in, was a well thumbed copy of On the Road. it has been Molly's favourite book since college, and the long she spend in this four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

She spent the last 10years in this room, sitting under a poster with an oscar wilde quote proclaimimg that work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do. Although many considered her pioneering work, unraviling the secret of Ilama DNA to be an outstanting achievement. Molly did think she had something better to do.
